About Moon HealthCare Group

Moon HealthCare Group Limited was founded to address unmet needs within the social care sector. Driven by a deep passion for supporting children and young people, founder Edu identified a critical opportunity : helping local authorities deliver bespoke care packages for those requiring emergency placements. With years of hands-on experience in children's services, Edu gained the insight and expertise needed to build a business that offers compliant, and child-centred unregulated placements for young people in crisis situations.
