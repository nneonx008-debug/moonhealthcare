About Us

We specialise in emergency placements for children and young people, providing immediate, short-term care when it’s needed most. Whether a child is at risk of harm, facing abandonment, or experiencing a sudden breakdown in their current living situation, we step in quickly—often overnight or over a weekend—without the long planning processes typical of standard placements. Our approach is highly flexible. A placement can last anywhere from a single night to several weeks, depending on the child’s needs and circumstances. Throughout this critical time, our priority is to offer safety, protection, and calm in the middle of crisis. We help children stabilise emotionally and practically after trauma or distress, and where appropriate, we provide therapeutic support to help them cope with the uncertainty they may be feeling. At the same time, we work closely with social services, families, and long-term carers to ensure a smooth transition into stable, ongoing care. Our goal is to be a compassionate first response—delivering safety and support when children are most vulnerable, while building a bridge toward a more permanent solution. Every emergency placement we provide includes full management oversight from our Package Managers, who personally meet each child or young person to build genuine, trusting support. They are always available should a child want to talk about their care or raise any concern. Our Package Managers take a hands-on approach, carrying out regular in-person placement visits, reviewing all paperwork and notes, attending care meetings, liaising with social workers and other professionals, and consistently supervising support workers to ensure the highest standard of care.
