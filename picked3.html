We specialise in emergency placements, providing immediate, short-term care for children needing safety from harm, abandonment, or care disruptions. Often arranged overnight or on weekends without standard planning, these placements can last from one night to several weeks. We offer immediate protection, crisis management, and sometimes therapeutic care while long-term arrangements are made.

Services - Bespoke Care.

Every emergency package we provide includes full management oversight from our Package Managers who personally meets each child or young person to build genuine support. Our package managers are always available to them should they wish to discuss their care or any concerns, and are hands on to deliver ongoing oversight through regular in-person placement visits, quality checks of all paperwork and notes, attendance at care meetings, liaison with social workers and other professionals, and consistent supervision of Support Workers.
